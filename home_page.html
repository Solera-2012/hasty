Sex Trafficking:
A relationship in which one person is controlled through violence, the threat of violence, or psychological coercion, has lost free will and free movement, is explored economically and paid nothing beyond subsistence.
Human sex trafficking is a modern-day form of slavery that involves the illegal trade of people for sexual exploitation or commercial gain. A topic that is not easy to talk about, and even harder to believe that it happens here in Portland metro area more than we would like to believe. According to the U.S Department of Justice about 300,000 American youths are trafficked for sexual exploitation. More and more data is being released about this terrible topic, the language around sex trafficking is changing, and the punishment towards the victims is slowly shifting to the men and women recruiting youth.
